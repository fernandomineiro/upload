RADIO MEMORY AI API

Artificial intelligence tools specialized in analyzing dental X-Ray and images

Add advanced computer vision capabilities to your dental programs

RM's dental computer vision system uses artificial intelligence techniques and specialized knowledge to automatically classify, segment, group, cut and align images, as well as locate, identify, measure and describe elements within them. These inferences are used to produce automatic and semi-automatic diagnostic and reports, provided to different specialties for analysis and treatment planning.

The services are made available through an API that can be accessed over the Internet and is designed to power computer programs for dentists with resources of automatic image analysis.

The API has powered Radio Memory's dental systems such as Radiocef Studio 3 and IDOC since June/2019. They are used monthly by around 100,000 dentists in Brazil, Latin America and Europe, and have performed well in more than 2 million cases of real patients.

Dental insurance operators also use the API in their programs to automate steps in expertise and fraud prevention.

API Calls are simple and straightforward, usually receiving one or more images and returning the inferred description.
